Get Started

Add Text to Photos and Bring New Meaning to Your Images

Our text editor comes packed full of fonts, styles, and everything else you'll need to add text to photos!

Get Started

Our Fonts

We provide plenty of extra funky fonts in our Text Editor that you won't be able to find anywhere else. Hammer it out with Carbontype, an old-fashioned typeface that will give your photos a classic look and feel that's sure to charm. Curious about our Luckiest Guy font? The name says it all. This lively looking font is perfect for adding a fun, quirky message to your photos. And with over 50 different fonts, you'll be able to find the perfect one for every occasion.

Your Fonts

These fonts might seem a little more familiar. Our Text Editor gives you full access to all the fonts on your device. Go for everyone's favorite, Helvetica, which looks great anywhere and is always clear and easy to read. Keep it classic and use Times, which you can never go wrong with. Or explore the unknown, and install any font you'd like to your device and it'll be right there waiting for you in BeFunky's Text Editor.

Personalized Fonts

No need to learn Photoshop. The BeFunky Photo Editor offers premium text editing features, without any of the hassle. Add background color and make your font stand out, while complimenting the tones in your photograph. Use a text outline, and give it an inverted look. Apply text wrapping, and watch as the words flow around your image. Keep everything in position with snapping. Or, resize and rotate your typeface till it looks just right.

Add Text to Photos in Any Font

Whatever look you're going for, it's never been easier to add text to photos, designs, and collages than with our Text Editor.

Capture It

When you capture the perfect moment, you need the perfect font to go with it. This one will make a statement you can't ignore with big, bold, all capitals letters.

Dancing Script

Soft and elegant, this font will romanticize almost any photograph. Perfect for black and white pictures, it adds just the right amount of sophistication.

Luckiest Guy

This font has a lot of personality. It's fun, it's confident and it screams for attention. Use it next time you add text to a photo, and it will be sure to make an impact.

Carbon Type

Belonging to the typewriter family, this retro font is the perfect accent to your vintage-style photographs. It might even make you feel a little nostalgic.

American Typewriter

Hammer it out with this old fashioned text, and give your next snapshot a classic look and feel that will be sure to charm.

Komika Axis

Always easy to read, this striking font is simple enough for most projects, yet still has an edge. You can be confident that it will look great anywhere.

02.

Customize Text

03.

Transform Text

04.

Click 'Flatten Layers' when you're done customizing your text.
